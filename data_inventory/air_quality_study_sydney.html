26 Air_Quality_Study_Sydney

Project metadata
Project Title Air_Quality_Study_Sydney
Owners NSW Office of Environment and Heritage
Project Abstract Multi-year study, starting in 2016, drawing on data from the Sydney Air Quality Monitoring Network, the NSW Greater Metropolitan Regional Air Emissions Inventory, and from research studies such as the Sydney Particle Study and the Sydney Particle Characterisation Study. A regional airshed model was established to examine air quality and population exposures variation and to quantify major source contributions to air pollution and exposure. Future projections of air quality changes and consequent impacts are also studied using this model.
